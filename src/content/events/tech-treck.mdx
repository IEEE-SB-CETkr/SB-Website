## About the Event

TECH TRECK was a project idea competition conducted as part of La Guerre 2.0, in collaboration with the IEEE IA/IE/PELS Joint Chapter Kerala. The event focused on socially relevant topics and received innovative entries from participants across multiple institutions.

TECH TRECK had several compelling ideas, making it a challenge to determine the winners. The winners were Sayooj Rajan from Vimal Jyothi Engineering College Chemperi, who secured first place, and Harikesh O P from Government College of Engineering Kannur, who won the second prize. The event concluded successfully with certificates awarded to all winners.

## Event Details

- Number of participants: 10
- Date: 10th October 2024
- Venue: CETkr Campus

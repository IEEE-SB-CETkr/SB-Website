## About the Event

IEEE Power and Energy Society (PES) SB Chapter, College of Engineering Trikaripur, conducted an online 'Quiz Competition' on 25th April 2024 from 7:00 PM to 7:30 PM as part of PES Day 2024. The event was held via Google Forms and focused on the theme 'Empowering Electric Mobility Innovation.'

The quiz was open to all students, and questions were designed to test knowledge and awareness about electric mobility and its innovations. Online registration began two days prior to the event, resulting in 70 registrations. All further details and updates were communicated through a dedicated WhatsApp group for participants.

## Event Highlights

- Date: 25th April 2024
- Time: 7:00 PM  7:30 PM
- Venue: Google Forms (Online)
- Number of participants: 70
- Theme: Empowering Electric Mobility Innovation

The competition saw enthusiastic participation, with students showcasing their knowledge and interest in the field. The winners were:

- **1st Place:** Nandhu Satheesan (S2 ECE, College of Engineering Trikaripur)
- **2nd Place:** Liya Latheesh (S2 ECE, College of Engineering Trikaripur)

Participation certificates were awarded to all participants, and the winners were recognized for their outstanding performance.

## About the Event

Join us for IEEE Day 2023, a global celebration of engineers and their contributions to society. This year's theme is "Leading the Next Wave of Innovation," focusing on emerging technologies and their impact on our future.

- **10:30 AM - 11:30 AM**: Inaugural Session
- **11:45 AM - 1:15 PM**: Technical Workshops

- **5:45 PM**: Valedictory & Prize Distribution

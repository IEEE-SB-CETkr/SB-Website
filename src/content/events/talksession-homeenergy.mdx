## About the Event

A talk session on Home Energy Audit was held on 6th September 2024 at the EEE Seminar Hall. The session was handled by Mr. Adil Nasser, Assistant Professor, EEE Department. The session was engaging and informative, with a total of 42 participants.

The event commenced at 1:30 PM, and attendees found the content insightful and beneficial. The session concluded with feedback delivered by some students, highlighting the value and impact of the session.

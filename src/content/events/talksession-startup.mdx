## About the Event

IEEE WIE SBC CETkr conducted a talk session on startups on 3rd April, 2024 at 8:30 PM via Google Meet. The IEEE WIE Kerala Section organized this session titled “Start-ups” as part of WIEmerge Week 2024, celebrating Women’s Day by bringing together WIE Affinity Groups from multiple institutions, including LBSCEK, ASET, GECK, and CETkr.

The session was designed to inspire and educate attendees on the intricacies of starting and managing a business, encouraging innovation and entrepreneurial spirit among students and professionals. Collaboration among multiple IEEE WIE Affinity Groups made the event more impactful.

## Event Highlights

- Poster and content were shared to the public 3 days prior to the talk session.
- The 1-hour session was informative and helpful for gaining new ideas.
- Feedback and suggestions were collected from attendees.
- Participants were required to register via a provided Google Forms link.
- 100 participants were included in the session.
- Contact details were shared for addressing queries, ensuring smooth coordination.
- Certificates were provided to participants to acknowledge their involvement.

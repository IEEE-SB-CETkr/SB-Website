## About the Event

IEEE PES Kerala Chapter, in association with IEEE Women in Power Kerala, IEEE R10 PES Women in Power, IEEE Women in Engineering, and IEEE PES SBC CETkr, conducted the first talk session of the Women Of Wisdom (WOW 5.0) Tech-Talk Series on 10th May 2024. The session, titled ‘Estimation of solar radiation and its impact on transition to green energy’, was handled by Dr. Kumari Namrata, Associate Professor, Dept. of Electrical Engineering, NIT Jamshedpur.

The event was open to all IEEE as well as non-IEEE members and saw the participation of over 35 attendees. The session provided valuable insights into the estimation of solar radiation and its crucial role in the transition to green energy. The poster and content were shared to the public one week prior to the talk session, and all details were communicated to participants via WhatsApp.

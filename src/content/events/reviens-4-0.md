## About the Event

IEEE SB CETKR proudly hosted **Reviens 4.0**, tagged _Sparkles of Inspiration_, from November 1st to 3rd, 2024. This flagship three-day event was meticulously curated to provide a blend of technical wisdom and cultural vibrancy. The event featured specialized workshops, inspiring professional talks, engaging games, diverse activities, and colourful programs.

We were honored to have **214 delegates** join us on this remarkable journey of learning and networking. The event was supported by a dedicated team of **50 volunteers** who worked tirelessly to ensure its success.

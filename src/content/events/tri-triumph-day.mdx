## About the Event

IEEE PES SBC LBSCEK and IEEE PES SBC CETkr jointly organized a 3-day online challenge, 'TRI-DAY TRIUMPH', held from 11th to 13th July 2024. The event was conducted through Google Forms and aimed to engage students in a series of competitive activities over three consecutive days.

Online registration began 6 days prior to the event, resulting in 60 registrations. All further details and updates were communicated to participants via a dedicated WhatsApp group.

## Event Highlights

- Dates: 11th, 12th, and 13th July 2024
- Venue: Google Forms (Online)
- Number of participants: 60
- Organized by: IEEE PES SBC LBSCEK & IEEE PES SBC CETkr

The winners of the challenge were:
- **Anshif** (College Of Engineering Trikaripur)
- **Jaazima Abdul Khader** (Vimal Jyothi College Of Engineering, Chemperi)
- **Fidha Ahamed** (Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam)

Participation certificates were awarded to all participants, and the winners were recognized for their outstanding performance.

## About the Event

IEEE SB CETKR is proud to present **Inizio 11.0**, the 11th edition of our flagship orientation and commencement ceremony. As we turn the page to a new chapter, Inizio stands as a beacon of new beginnings, bringing together the vibrant community of student members, distinguished faculty, and alumni.

This event marks not just the inauguration of the new executive committee but the celebration of a legacy of technical excellence and community spirit. It serves as a platform to inspire the incoming batch of engineers, instilling in them the core values of IEEE: innovation, collaboration, and professional growth. We invite everyone to witness this grand unfolding of aspirations and to be part of a journey that promises to be both enriching and transformative.

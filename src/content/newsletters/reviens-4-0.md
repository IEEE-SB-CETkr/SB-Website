# Reviens 4.0: Sparkles of Inspiration

**Date:** 1st - 3rd November 2024  
**Venue:** College of Engineering Trikaripur

IEEE SB CETKR proudly hosted **Reviens 4.0**, tagged _Sparkles of Inspiration_, from November 1st to 3rd, 2024. This flagship three-day event was meticulously curated to provide a blend of technical wisdom and cultural vibrancy, featuring relevant workshops, inspiring professional talks, engaging games, diverse activities, and colourful programs. We were honored to have **214 delegates** join us on this remarkable journey of learning and networking.

## Day 1: The Grand Beginning

The event commenced on Friday, 1st November 2024, with delegates arriving by 2:00 PM and completing the registration process by 4:00 PM. The smooth execution of the event was made possible by a dedicated team of **50 volunteers**, who served as the backbone of the program and worked tirelessly to ensure its success.

The formal inauguration ceremony began at 5:00 PM. **Mr. Vyshnav**, Chair (2024 Execom) of IEEE SB CETKR, delivered the welcome speech, setting a tone of warmth and enthusiasm. This was followed by the presentation of the **Code of Ethics** by **Ms. Hiba Hassan**, Secretary.

We were privileged to have the **Inaugural Address** delivered by **Dr. Mahesh VV**, Principal of the College of Engineering Trikaripur. The **Presidential Address** was given by **Dr. Naveena AK**, HOD of the Computer Science Department and Branch Counsellor of IEEE SB CETKR, who shared valuable insights with the gathering.

The audience was then addressed with a Felicitation by **Mr. Srikanth P.**, Assistant Professor from the EEE Department. The formal proceedings concluded with a Vote of Thanks proposed by **Ms. Bismi P Biju**, Vice Chair, expressing gratitude to all dignitaries and participants.

Following the inaugural ceremony, delegates transitioned into an engaging **Ice Breaking Session**, where they actively participated in a series of interactive games designed to foster camaraderie. The first day concluded on a high note with a vibrant **Campfire Night**, creating lasting memories for all attendees.

## Day 2: Technical Excellence and Celebration

The second day was dedicated to skill development and technical enrichment, featuring three simultaneous workshops tailored for different engineering streams:

- **Civil Engineering:** A specialized workshop on **AutoCAD**.
- **Computer Science:** A hands-on session on **Machine Learning Using Python**.
- **ECE & EEE:** A technical session on **Circuit Design and Simulation using Proteus**.

These workshops provided worthwhile and interesting practical knowledge to the students.

As the sun set, the focus shifted from technical skills to social engagement. The delegates enjoyed a spirited **Halloween-themed Party**, adding a unique festive flair to the event. The day concluded with fun games and extensive networking opportunities, allowing delegates to connect with peers across disciplines.

## Day 3: A Mesmerizing Finale

The final day proved to be a mesmerizing experience for both delegates and volunteers. The morning session featured a powerful talk by **Mr. Ajay Thomas**, CEO of Vatsaa Energy. His session, titled _Entrepreneurship Unleashed: Turning Ideas into Impact_, inspired the audience to think beyond conventional boundaries.

This was followed by an enthralling **Live Mentalism Performance** by **Mr. Vimal VP**, a renowned hypnotist and mentalist. His performance captivated the audience and provided a truly unique experience for everyone present.

The event drew to a close with a **Feedback Session**, where delegates openly shared their positive experiences and reflections on the three-day journey. The program officially concluded with a group **Photo Session**, capturing the smiles and spirit of the Reviens 4.0 community.

Overall, Reviens 4.0 was a resounding success, leaving every participant with new skills, fond memories, and sparkles of inspiration.
